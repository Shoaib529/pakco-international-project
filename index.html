About Us

Pakco International, we are a leading export company specializing in high-quality sheep casings for the global meat industry. With 25 years of experience in this industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted supplier of natural sheep casings, catering to the needs of meat processors and producers worldwide. Our commitment to quality and reliability is reflected in every aspect of our business. We source our casings from the finest sheep farms, ensuring premium quality that meets the strictest standards. Through our extensive network of partners and suppliers, we can offer a wide range of sizes and grades, tailored to the specific requirements of our customers. With a dedicated team of professionals, we pride ourselves on offering exceptional customer service and technical expertise. Whether you are a large-scale producer or a small butcher, we work closely with our clients to provide tailored solutions, timely deliveries, and superior products. At Pakco International, we are committed to building lasting relationships based on trust, integrity, and the consistent delivery of high-quality products. Our mission is to be the go-to choice for sheep casings globally, continuously innovating and expanding our operations to better serve our customers’ needs.
